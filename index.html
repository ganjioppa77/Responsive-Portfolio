About Me

Timothy Kim is a Loan officer at Bank of Hope, where he processes loan applications from start to finish, including commercial loan refinancing and educating clients about their different financing options. He helps commercial property owners and buyers secure a loan that suits their budget and goals.

Before working as a Loan officer, Timothy developed his financial analytic skills and logical thinking by majoring Business Administration in University of Southern California. He concentrated on Commercial and Corporate Finance to further pursuit in this career.

However, Timothy is recently interested in Fintech. He always enjoys learning computer technology. Throughout his undergraduate years, he learned basic Python and Advanced Excel. He is currently taking a Coding Bootcamp from UCLA extension to enhance his computer technology skills, and with that, he wants to pursuit a career in Fintech industry where he can use both of his finance and computer technology skills.
